About me

I am a data scientist with over 20 years consulting experience helping businesses solve strategic and operational issues. I currently work for Faegre Drinker, an international top 50 American law firm.

I was born in Senegal where I received the first part of my schooling ending with a Bachelor of Science in Statistics. I moved to the United States to further my education culminating with a Master of Science in Statistics and PhD in Economics from the University of Connecticut, Storrs, CT.

I started my professional career in Senegal as a Research and Teaching Assistant at ENEA (National School of Applied Economics), Dakar. My career really took off after my graduate studies when I joined Mercer Management Consulting in Lexington, MA.

Coming from a modest background, I am thankful for what I achieved to date and hopeful for the future. I live in New Jersey with my family and visit Senegal regularly.

In my spare time, among other activities, I like to:
  • play and watch soccer and coach little kids
  • enjoy nature: camping, short hikes, contemplation of beautiful sceneries
  • spend time with close friends
  • learn new things: currently I am into full stack web development and the Arabic language
I am fluent in Wolof, French and English.
